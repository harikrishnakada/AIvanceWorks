### 6.4 Client Engagement Models
**Fixed-Price Projects**
Ideal for well-defined projects with clear scope and deliverables. Client pays agreed-upon price regardless of hours invested. Includes change request process for scope modifications.
**Best For:** Specific features, migrations, MVPs with clear requirements

**Time & Materials (T&M)**
Flexible model where client pays for actual hours worked at agreed rates. Provides maximum flexibility for evolving requirements and exploratory work.
**Best For:** R&D projects, ongoing development, projects with uncertain scope

**Dedicated Team**
Client gets exclusive access to dedicated engineers for extended period. Team becomes extension of client's organization. Monthly rates with minimum 3-month commitment.
**Best For:** Long-term projects, product development, sustained development needs

**Retainer Model**
Pre-paid monthly hours at discounted rates. Guaranteed availability and priority response. Unused hours may roll over (terms negotiable).
**Best For:** Ongoing support, maintenance, continuous improvement, advisory services

**Hybrid Model**
Combination of fixed-price deliverables with T&M for ongoing enhancements. Provides predictability with flexibility.
**Best For:** Phased projects, platforms requiring initial build then continuous evolution
