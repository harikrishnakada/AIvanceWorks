# Team, Roles & Bios

## 7. TEAM COMPOSITION, ROLES & BIOS
Our team is structured to provide end-to-end delivery capabilities from strategy to execution. We provide staff augmentation and full delivery teams tailored to the project's scope.

### Core Team Roles
- **Solutions Architect** — System design, architecture governance, solution reviews, cloud strategy
- **Senior Full-Stack Engineer** — End-to-end development, technical mentorship
- **AI/ML Specialist** — Model selection, RAG, agentic systems, prompt engineering
- **DevOps Engineer** — CI/CD, infrastructure automation, monitoring
- **Product Owner / BA** — Requirement gathering, stakeholder engagement, backlog management
- **QA Lead / Engineer** — Test strategies, automation, performance testing
- **UI/UX Designer** — Design systems, user journeys, accessibility
- **Data Engineer / Analyst** — Data pipeline design, BI, analytics

### Example Team Configurations
- Small Project (MVP): 1 Architect (part-time), 2 Engineers, 1 QA, 1 PO — 8–12 weeks
- Medium Project: 1 Architect, 3 Engineers, 1 QA, 1 PO, 1 DevOps — 12–24 weeks
- Large Project (Enterprise): 1–2 Architects, 6+ Engineers, 2 QA, 1–2 DevOps, Technical PM — 6–12+ months
