# Client Engagement & Delivery Model

## 9. CLIENT ENGAGEMENT & DELIVERY MODEL
### Engagement Lifecycle
- Discovery & Assessment: Business objectives, technical audit, roadmap creation
- Planning: Detailed scope, milestones, and delivery timelines
- Execution: Agile sprints, CI/CD, and incremental releases
- Validation: QA, security review, performance testing
- Support & Handover: Documentation, training, and knowledge transfer

**Phase 1: Discovery & Assessment (1-2 weeks)**
**Activities:**
- Initial consultation and requirements gathering
- Technical assessment of existing systems
- Architecture review and recommendations
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Team composition and resource planning

**Deliverables:**
- Technical assessment report
- Proposed solution architecture
- Detailed project plan and timeline
- Fixed-price proposal or T&M estimate
- Risk register and mitigation strategies

**Cost:** $5,000 - $15,000 (credited toward project if engagement proceeds)

**Phase 2: Planning & Design (1-3 weeks)**
**Activities:**
- Detailed requirements documentation
- Technical design and architecture finalization
- Sprint planning and backlog creation
- Development environment setup
- Security and compliance review

**Phase 3: Development & Testing (project-dependent)**
Agile Methodology:
- 2-week sprint cycles
- Regular sprint demos and stakeholder reviews
- Continuous integration and deployment
- Automated testing and quality assurance
- Weekly status reports and metrics

**Phase 4: Deployment & Launch (1-2 weeks)**
**Activities:**
- Production environment setup
- Data migration (if applicable)
- User acceptance testing
- Training and documentation
- Go-live support

**Phase 5: Support & Optimization (30 days included)**
**Post-Launch Support:**
- Bug fixes and minor adjustments (included)
- Performance monitoring and optimization
- User support and issue resolution
- Knowledge transfer to client team

### SLAs & Support
- Standard SLA: 99.9% availability for managed services
- Premium SLA: 99.99% availability with on-call support
- Support models: Business hours, 24/7 (optional)

### Communication Cadence
- Weekly sprint demos, bi-weekly executive reviews, daily standups (as needed), and status reports.
