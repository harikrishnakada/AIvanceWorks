# Website Content Strategy

## 12. WEBSITE CONTENT STRATEGY
We recommend a modular website structure with dedicated pages for Services, Technology, Pricing, Team, Case Studies, Blog/Insights, and Contact.

### Core Website Pages
- Home/Index
- Company (About): mission, vision, values
- Services: Service detail pages per capability area
- Technology: Tech stack and certifications
- Pricing: Typical rates and engagement models
- Team: Bios and roles
- Case Studies: Project outcomes and metrics
- Blog/Insights: Thought leadership and technical deep-dives
- Contact: Engagement inquiry form and company details

### SEO & Content Strategy
- Focus on long-tail keywords for AI consulting, cloud migration, LLM integration, and Azure AI services
- Publish technical case studies and performance metrics to target decision-makers and technical leads
- Regular blog posts on practical AI adoption, migration patterns, and security best practices
