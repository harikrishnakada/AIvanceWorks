# Sales Proposal & Commercials

## 11. SALES PROPOSAL & COMMERCIALS
### Proposal Template
- Executive summary
- Scope of work and deliverables
- Project team and roles
- Timeline and milestones
- Pricing and payment terms
- SLAs and support terms
- Assumptions and exclusions
- Confidentiality and IP clauses

### Sales Process
- Lead qualification, technical discovery, demo/proof-of-concept, proposal delivery, negotiation, and close.
