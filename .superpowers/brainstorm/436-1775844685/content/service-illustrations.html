My Recommendation

A + B + C — Spot illustrations give visual richness to feature sections, animated scroll reveals make the process sections feel alive, and interactive signatures make the page memorable. Skip D (hero illustration) for now — the metrics card is earning its keep on service pages, and you can always revisit.

This combination keeps the text-only hero with metrics (which works for B2B proof), while making the rest of the page feel engaging and premium.
